Project Seaborn

About the project

Project Seaborn is an action RPG with a unique battle system that I'm developing in the Unity Game Engine. Its still early on in development, but I plan to have a variety of weapons and jobs that you can mix and match to create a party to fit your playstyle.

The battle system has two phases, the planning phase and the battle phase. During the planning phase, you perform turn based actions in order gain an advantage. In the battle phase, you enter into bullet hell combat where you can make use of your selected party member's unique weapons and skills.

You can follow development through my devlog series.
