What I Do

Hello, I’m Levin and I make games. On my YouTube channel, ButWhyLevin, I make devlogs covering the development of my game projects as well as video essay projects from time to time. This website is a hub for all of my creations and my social medias.

Past Projects

My previous main project was a Splitscreen FPS developed in Unity named “Couch Combat.” Through its development, I learned Unity and 3D modeling, and I also made numerous smaller projects and game jams on the side while I was at work on Couch Combat. Now that Couch Combat is complete and released on Steam, I am currently working on an Action RPG called Project Seaborn. You can learn more about all of these projects through their respective pages on this website and my devlogs covering them on my YouTube channel.
